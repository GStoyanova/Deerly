About Us


Deerly stands for natural, sustainable, compassionate and reliable produce. We approach the cosmetic world and add a vegan, cruelty free and organic touch to it.

Our vision says it all: “In a world where we are surrounded by harmful chemicals and plastic produce, we strongly believe that we must take action for a more sustainable world, only then we can live a more natural and healthy life”.




Our mission is to allow our customers to take care of their skin while taking care of the nature as well. By choosing Deerly, you choose to help the environment.

What makes our products distinctive is that we use only organic ingredients, and our packaging is recycable, made out of paper, cardboard and glass.

Our social media campaign aims to increase awareness on important environmental issues and persuades people to take actions to a more sustainable and cruelty-free world.
